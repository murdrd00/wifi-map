What do dBm values mean?

-30 to -40Excellent — streaming, video calls
-40 to -55Good — reliable for most tasks
-55 to -70Fair — browsing OK, may lag
-70 to -85Weak — spotty connection
-85 to -100Dead zone — no usable signal

dBm (decibels relative to milliwatt) measures WiFi signal power. Values closer to 0 are stronger. Each 3 dBm increase doubles the power.
